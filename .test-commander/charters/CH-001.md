## Mission

Exercise the create-appointment workflow end to end: department-driven doctor filtering, validation rules (past date, inactive doctor/patient, doctor-department mismatch), status transitions, and dashboard reflection.

## Target Area

Appointment

## Time-Box

60min. Within this time-box the exploration should produce at least one observation per acceptance criterion and capture screenshots at every significant page transition.

## Risk Areas

- Authentication / authorization boundaries
- Session lifecycle and token leakage
- Performance under documented load thresholds
- Input validation on user-supplied data

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Selecting a department filters the doctor dropdown to that department and refreshes when the department changes.
2. Creating an appointment with a valid patient, doctor, department, future date and time succeeds.
3. An appointment with a past date is rejected with a validation error.
4. A doctor outside the selected department is rejected for the appointment.
5. Appointment status can transition to Checked In, Completed, Cancelled, and No Show.
6. A new appointment appears in the list and dashboard counts update; a cancelled appointment is not counted as upcoming.
